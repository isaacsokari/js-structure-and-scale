D. Palmieri's Bakery

Try a unique Rhode Island delicacy: pizza strips. No, they're not just marinara and bread. Yes, they're frickin' delicious. Don't get confused and visit J. Palmieri's Bakery (also in Johnston), or Palmieri's Bakery (in Providence). Totally different places, nowhere near as good.

It's located at 624 Killingly Street, Johston, RI, 02919
